Old School Runescape

Old School RuneScape is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game developed and published by Jagex. The game was first released on 22 February 2013 in response to the declining playerbase and negative update reception of the original and then-current version of RuneScape. OSRS was originally an exact copy of RuneScape as it was in August 2007, but has since received engine improvements, new content, and quality of life patches. Proposed gameplay updates to the game are voted on by players, and are only implemented if 75% of paying players accept them. Despite having a smaller staff and a slower relative update schedule, OSRS had more than twice the active players that RuneScape had by 2017. A mobile version of the game for Android and iOS was released on 30 October 2018.
